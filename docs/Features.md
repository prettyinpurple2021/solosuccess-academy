# Features

SoloSuccess Academy provides a rich, interactive learning experience built around the needs of solo founders. This page documents every major feature available on the platform.

---

## Student Features

### 📚 10-Course Curriculum
A structured learning path divided into three phases — Initialization, Orchestration, and Launch Sequence — guiding students from foundational mindset work through market research, branding, automation, content strategy, financial bootstrapping, sales psychology, strategic roadmapping, and final pitch delivery. Each course consists of multiple lessons, assignments, and a graded quiz.

See [Curriculum](Curriculum.md) for the full course and lesson listing.

---

### 🤖 AI Tutor
An intelligent, conversational tutoring assistant available on every lesson page. Students can ask questions, request explanations, or explore ideas in real time. The AI Tutor maintains context within a session, making it feel like a personal mentor rather than a generic chatbot.

---

### 📖 Interactive Textbooks
Each course includes a flip-book style digital textbook. Students can:
- Highlight passages and save highlights to their personal notes
- Add margin notes tied to specific pages
- Navigate chapter-by-chapter with smooth page-turn animations

---

### 🃏 Flashcard System
A built-in spaced-repetition flashcard system powered by the **SM-2 algorithm** ensures students review content at scientifically optimal intervals. Flashcards are generated from lesson content and can also be created manually.

---

### 📝 Quizzes & Graded Assessments
Every course concludes with a graded quiz. Quizzes feature:
- Multiple-choice questions with instant feedback
- Score tracking and progress toward a passing grade
- A final comprehensive exam required for certification

---

### ✍️ Worksheets & Assignments
Practical, hands-on assignments appear throughout the curriculum. Students submit written work directly on the platform and receive AI-powered feedback alongside manual instructor review.

---

### 💬 Discussion Forums
Threaded community forums are available per lesson, allowing students to:
- Post questions and responses
- Reply to peers
- Receive notifications when their threads receive replies

---

### 🎮 Gamification System
Learning is rewarded through a gamification layer that tracks and displays:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **XP (Experience Points)** | Earned by completing lessons, quizzes, assignments, and daily logins |
| **Streaks** | Consecutive days of activity tracked and celebrated |
| **Badges** | Unlockable achievements for milestones (e.g., first course completed, 7-day streak) |
| **Leaderboard** | A live ranking of all students by total XP |

---

### 📜 Verified Certificates
Upon completing all lessons and passing the course quiz, students receive an auto-generated PDF Certificate of Completion. Each certificate includes:
- Student's full name
- Course title and completion date
- A unique verification code (format: `SSA-XXXX-XXXX`)
- A public verification URL where anyone can confirm the certificate's authenticity

---

### 🔔 Notifications
An in-app notification system alerts students to:
- New replies in their discussion threads
- Assignment grading updates
- Course completion milestones

---

### 👤 Student Profile
Each student has a profile page displaying:
- Display name and avatar
- Enrolled courses and progress percentages
- Earned badges and certificates
- XP total and streak count

---

## Admin Features

### 📋 Course Management
Administrators can create, edit, and organize courses, lessons, and all associated content through a dedicated Admin Dashboard.

---

### 🤖 AI Content Generator
Admins can generate lesson text, quiz questions, worksheet prompts, and discussion starters using integrated AI generation tools. The generator supports customizable tone, length, and template options to maintain a consistent voice across all content.

---

### 📊 Student Analytics
A real-time analytics dashboard surfaces key engagement metrics:
- Enrollment counts per course
- Lesson completion rates
- Quiz score distributions
- Student activity over time

---

### 📒 Gradebook
Administrators can review and manually grade student assignment submissions alongside AI-generated feedback scores.

---

## Platform-Wide Features

### 🔐 Security
- **Row Level Security (RLS)** — All database records are protected at the row level; students can only access their own data
- **Email Authentication** — Secure email/password login with email verification
- **Rate Limiting** — AI endpoints are rate-limited to prevent abuse

### 🌐 Responsive Design
The platform is fully responsive and optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers.

### 🎨 Theme
SoloSuccess Academy uses a cyberpunk-inspired dark visual theme with neon cyan and purple accents, creating an energetic and focused learning environment.
