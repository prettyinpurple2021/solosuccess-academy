# Curriculum

SoloSuccess Academy delivers a 10-course entrepreneurship curriculum structured across three progressive phases. This page is the canonical reference for all courses, lessons, and interactive activities.

---

## Curriculum Overview

| Phase | # | Course | Description |
|-------|---|--------|-------------|
| **Phase 1 – Initialization** | 1 | The Solo Singularity – Mindset & Vision | Build the mental foundation for solo entrepreneurship. Develop your unique founder identity, harness AI-powered productivity techniques, and set the strategic vision that will guide your business from day one. |
| **Phase 1 – Initialization** | 2 | Signal in the Noise – AI Market Intelligence | Use AI tools to cut through market noise and find a winning business idea. Master social listening, automated research, and smoke-test validation techniques without spending a dollar. |
| **Phase 1 – Initialization** | 3 | Neon Identity – AI-Powered Branding | Craft a striking, professional brand identity using AI design tools. Develop your visual language, brand voice, and trust signals to stand out in a crowded market from day one. |
| **Phase 2 – Orchestration** | 4 | The Ghost Machine – Workflow Automation | Build automated systems that run your business while you sleep. Learn no-code tools to automate communication, scheduling, customer management, and onboarding flows. |
| **Phase 2 – Orchestration** | 5 | The Infinite Loop – The Content Multiplier | Create a content engine that turns one idea into a month of marketing material. Master AI repurposing workflows, automated scheduling, and platform-specific strategies to grow your audience on autopilot. |
| **Phase 2 – Orchestration** | 6 | Digital Gravity – Attracting Your Audience | Build an owned audience that fuels sustainable growth. Learn list-building, lead-magnet creation, warm outreach, and SEO fundamentals to attract loyal customers without paid ads. |
| **Phase 2 – Orchestration** | 7 | Zero-Point Energy – Financial Bootstrapping | Master the financial fundamentals of a profitable solo business. Learn smart pricing, the barter economy, the best free-tier tools, and how to audit expenses to maximize profit from day one. |
| **Phase 3 – Launch Sequence** | 8 | The Neuro-Link – Psychology of Sales | Rewire your relationship with selling and master persuasion science. Learn copywriting frameworks, objection handling, and follow-up sequences to close deals with confidence. |
| **Phase 3 – Launch Sequence** | 9 | Future State – Strategic Roadmapping | Plan the long-term future of your business with strategic clarity. Set measurable OKRs, learn when to pivot vs. persevere, and protect your digital assets against risk. |
| **Phase 3 – Launch Sequence** | 10 | The Final Transmission – Storytelling & Pitch | Bring your entire journey together in a polished, investor-ready pitch. Master storytelling structure, slide design, video recording, and portfolio assembly for maximum impact. |

## Certificate of Completion

Students who complete all lessons in a course and pass the course quiz earn an official **SoloSuccess Academy Certificate of Completion**. Certificates include a unique verification code and can be independently verified at any time via the platform's public verification page.

The **Solo Founder Certificate** — awarded after passing the Final Exam in Course 10 — serves as the capstone credential for the full program.
