# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

## General

### What is SoloSuccess Academy?
SoloSuccess Academy is a private, AI-powered Learning Management System (LMS) designed specifically for solo founders and small business owners. It delivers a 10-course curriculum covering every aspect of launching and growing a one-person business — from mindset and branding to sales psychology and investor pitches.

---

### Who is SoloSuccess Academy for?
The platform is built for:
- **Aspiring entrepreneurs** who want to launch a business without a co-founder or external funding
- **Freelancers** looking to productize their skills and scale sustainably
- **Side-project founders** who want a structured path to turn an idea into a real business
- **Small business owners** who want to fill knowledge gaps in marketing, automation, or strategy

---

### How much does it cost?
Each course is priced at $49 and can be purchased individually. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.

---

### Is my data private?
Yes. All personal data and course progress are protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies at the database level. Students can only access their own records. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

---

## Learning Experience

### How does the AI Tutor work?
The AI Tutor is a conversational assistant available on every lesson page. You can ask it to explain concepts, clarify assignments, give examples, or help you brainstorm. Chat sessions are saved so you can return to previous conversations.

---

### What types of lessons are there?
SoloSuccess Academy offers six lesson types:
- **Text** — Rich written content
- **Video** — Video lessons with secure playback
- **Quiz** — Multiple-choice assessments with instant feedback
- **Assignment** — Written submissions with AI-powered feedback
- **Worksheet** — Multi-question structured exercises
- **Activity** — Step-by-step guided activities

---

### What is the Flashcard System?
The flashcard system uses the **SM-2 spaced-repetition algorithm** — the same method used by professional memory tools like Anki. Cards are shown at scientifically optimal intervals so you review material just before you're likely to forget it.

---

### How do quizzes work?
Each course includes graded quizzes with multiple-choice questions and instant feedback. Your score is saved and contributes to your overall course grade.

---

### What is the Final Exam?
Each course can include a comprehensive final exam covering key concepts. Exams support multiple-choice, true/false, and short-answer questions. Answers are graded server-side for security.

---

### What is the Final Essay?
Courses may include an essay assessment with multiple prompt options, rubric-based grading, and word limits. Essays receive both AI-generated and manual instructor scores.

---

### How do I earn a Certificate?
To earn a Certificate of Completion:
1. Complete all lessons in the course
2. Meet the graduation requirements (milestones, exams, etc.)

Your certificate is automatically generated as a downloadable PDF with a unique verification code (`SSA-XXXX-XXXX`).

---

### Can anyone verify my certificate?
Yes. Every certificate includes a unique verification code and a public verification URL. Anyone can visit that URL to confirm the certificate's authenticity.

---

### What is the Textbook?
Each course includes an interactive digital textbook with a flip-book interface. Features include text highlighting, bookmarks, inline comments, a vocabulary glossary, embedded quizzes, text-to-speech, flashcard generation, and an "Explain This" AI panel for deeper understanding.

---

## Gamification

### What is XP?
XP (Experience Points) is earned by completing lessons, passing quizzes, submitting assignments, and maintaining daily login streaks. Your total XP determines your position on the leaderboard.

---

### What are Streaks?
A streak counts consecutive days of activity. Keeping your streak alive earns bonus XP.

---

### What are Badges?
Badges are visual achievements awarded for reaching specific milestones — completing courses, maintaining streaks, or earning certificates.

---

## Account & Access

### How do I sign in?
You can sign in with **email and password** or use **Google Sign-In** for one-click access.

---

### How do I reset my password?
On the sign-in page, click **"Forgot password?"**, enter your email address, and follow the reset link in the email you receive.

---

### How do I update my profile?
Navigate to the **Profile** page from the sidebar. You can update your display name, avatar, and bio.

---

### What is the Notepad Widget?
A floating, draggable notepad available on every authenticated page. Use it to jot down ideas or notes while studying.
