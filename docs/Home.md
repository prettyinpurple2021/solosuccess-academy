## What Is SoloSuccess Academy?

SoloSuccess Academy is a comprehensive online education platform designed to take aspiring entrepreneurs from mindset and vision all the way through to a polished investor-ready pitch — entirely on their own, at their own pace.

The platform delivers a structured, 10-course curriculum across three progressive phases:

| Phase | Focus | Courses |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **Phase 1 – Initialization** | Identity & Intel | Courses 1–3 |
| **Phase 2 – Orchestration** | Building the Machine | Courses 4–7 |
| **Phase 3 – Launch Sequence** | Sales & Future | Courses 8–10 |

Each course combines six lesson types (text, video, quiz, assignment, worksheet, activity), interactive textbooks, AI-assisted learning, practice labs, capstone projects, final exams/essays, spaced-repetition flashcards, community discussions, and a verified certificate of completion.
